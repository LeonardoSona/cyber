    Identity Risk Story

    Focused view on access, authentication, and vendor-linked exposure.

    Current State

    Identity remains the highest-risk domain in Q1 2026, driven by gaps in MFA enforcement, recertification coverage, and legacy authentication on selected externally connected applications.

    Why It Matters

    This is the most direct path from technical weakness to business impact, because successful compromise through valid credentials can bypass many perimeter controls and expose regulated or sensitive datasets.

    Priority Actions

    • Accelerate MFA enforcement for privileged and external-facing accounts.
    • Complete migration of legacy vendor-connected authentication flows to OAuth2.
    • Automate third-party access recertification through ServiceNow workflow.
